Much of this section is written in Spanish, reflecting the source texts. Reading literature in its original language reveals nuances lost in translation.

== Purpose

Literature is not escape — it is deepening. These texts teach:

* **Cervantes**: The tension between idealism and reality
* **García Márquez**: The weight of history and the magic of the everyday
* **Reina Valera**: Wisdom, poetry, and transcendence
